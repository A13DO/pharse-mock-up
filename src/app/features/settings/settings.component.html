ℹ️ How to get your JWT token (Recommended):
  1. Log in to your Phrase account at cloud.memsource.com
  2. Go to Settings → Access & Security → Access Tokens
  3. Click Generate New Token
  4. Copy the JWT token
  5. Paste it in the field above
